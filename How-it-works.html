How it works

Post currently offers two ways to access
the app:

1. Go to postclientapp.web.app on a
mobile device browser

2. Go to postclientapp.web.app on a
desktop browser

Note: Post is still in very early development
and will be coming out with native apps in
the far future.

Then, a sign in will prompt you with
two options: Google or Outlook.

As of 3.13.2021, Outlook is not supported.

In the app

Post currently only supports viewing unread emails

Post is currently aiming for the following filter types:

  • unread
  • all
  • labels (important, starred, etc.)

Anyways, here are all the basics you need to get started.


Messages
To view a message, simply click on it and it will slide you
into a separate view, exclusive to the message — but not
a different webpage.


Actions
From the message list view, you can quick tap on an icon
to use that action.

You can also take actions from the expanded message
view (what you see when you click on an email).


Sidebar
To toggle the sidebar, click on the "Messages" text
near the top of the page. You can also close the sidebar
by clicking on the X near the bottom of the sidebar.

We are currently working on integrating your respective
task app (Google Tasks for Gmail, Microsoft To-Do for Outlook).
They will be accessible from the sidebar.



…and that’s pretty much it!

For now, check out the latest blogs on our status.
Building apps doesn’t happen overnight!
